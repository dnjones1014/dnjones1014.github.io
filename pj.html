HOA goals:
1. Continue preserving and maintaining the overall appeal and desirability of Prairie Fields, while being vigilant of issues or conditions that may affect the quality of living.
2. Maintain a healthy reserve in the Prairie Fields operations account, and collaborate on new ideas to improve our community, and increase property values.
3. See a permanent solution to the drainage field on the East end of Prairie Fields Park, along First St; perhaps a fish-stocked retention pond, for fishing derbies, paddle boating, and ice skating (in approved safety conditions).
